What are we?

Animation breathes life into still images, creating the illusion of movement through rapid sequences of frames. From hand-drawn classics to mind-blowing CGI, animation entertains, educates, and inspires audiences of all ages. Stop-motion brings puppets and objects to life, while 2D animation offers a spectrum of artistic styles. 3D animation takes viewers into fantastical worlds, and motion graphics add dynamic visuals to presentations and marketing. Today, animation is a powerful storytelling tool used in film, television, games, and even web design.
